ROVER SCOUT MANUAL & REGULATIONS

Welcome to the Rover Scouting Program of Galle District!
This syllabus covers senior scouts and young adults aged 17 to 26 years.

Pillars of Rovering:
1. Service (B-P's motto "Service"): Projects in nature conservation, education, and community support.
2. Self-Training: Vocational skills, outdoor leadership, advanced wood badge credentials.
3. Social Integration: Global scout networking, disaster mitigation, policy planning.
