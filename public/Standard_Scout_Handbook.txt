GALLE DISTRICT SCOUT BRANCH
STANDARD SCOUT TRAINING HANDBOOK

Welcome to the Standard Scouting Program of Galle District!
This syllabus covers scouts aged 11 to 17 years.

Core Competency Pillars:
1. Scouting Craft: Compass reading, mapping, pioneering lashings, shelter building.
2. Health & Safety: Complete first aid, resuscitation, emergency response.
3. Patrol Leadership: Camp management, patrol planning, scout community service.

Milestone Badges:
- Tenderfoot, Second Class, First Class, President's Scout Badge.
